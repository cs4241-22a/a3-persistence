To Do List

Create a Todo item with its due date and tag (if applicable)!

  • The second input field is for the due date. Put the date you wish to complete the todo item by.
  • Once you complete a todo item, click "Done" button to remove.
  • The column "Priority" shows red, yellow, or green depending on how many days are left until the due date.
    • Red: less than or equal to 1 day
    • Yellow: more than a day but less than 3 days
    • Green: more than 3 days
  • There are also five different tags available - webware, deepLearning, businessIntelligence, MQP, and work. They are color-coded differently.
