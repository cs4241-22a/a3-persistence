Welcome to my To-Do List. Please enter the name of the task, how long you think it will take to complete, as well as the date that the task needs to be completed by. The list will then rank the priority of each task based on the information you provide, giving you insight to your optimal order or operations.

To add a task to the list, fill out the correct information about the task and hit the GREEN Submit button. To delete a task which is already inside the table, make sure to correctly spell the desired task and then hit the RED Delete button.
